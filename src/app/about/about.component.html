About Us


“If the bee disappeared off the surface of the globe then man would only have four years of life left. No more bees, no more pollination, no more plants, no more animals, no more man.”

Albert Einstein

BeeCompanion is a platform/application which brings beekeepers and beefriends together. We offer various tools to maintain/organize your favourite hobby as beekeeper and help you to create a personal fanbase who are able to support you as a beekeeper and to raise donations.

Our goal is to connect interestet people with their local beekeepers and to fight bee death by supporting those local beekeepers with small but valuable donations.
